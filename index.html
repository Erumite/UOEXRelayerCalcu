Tips & Considerations

Relayer Wiki

Full details on the Relayer Wiki Page.

Plan for Gear Swaps

Keep critical stats off slots you may swap out. For example, Bracelets of Binding (wrist) are frequently equipped at champion spawns for teleportation — don’t put stats there that you can’t afford to lose.

Buff Resist Penalties

Protection and Vampiric Embrace reduce resistances. If you keep them active, plan extra resists to compensate. Toggling these buffs adjusts resist caps in the character sheet; the status bar caps at 70 (the actual in-game maximum).

Physical Resist & Reflect

You may not want to max Physical Resistance — Reflect Physical Damage scales off the physical damage you take. More damage taken means more reflected. Just make sure you have the HP and healing to survive.

Regen Diminishing Returns

There is no hard cap on HP/Mana/Stamina regen, but returns diminish sharply beyond the configured cap. See the diminishing returns thread for details.

Recommended on Every Relayer

These attributes are worth maxing on every armor relayer (use the Prep button to set them all at once):

  • +8 STR (32 SP) — hit harder, more HP. No cap.
  • +8 DEX (32 SP) — faster healing (see 220 dex), more stamina. No cap.
  • +8 INT (32 SP) — more mana, better spell success. No cap.
  • +8 HP (24 SP) — survivability. No cap.
  • +8 Stamina (24 SP) — sustain swing speed through leeches. No cap.
  • +8 Mana (24 SP) — fuel weapon specials (Whirlwind, Double Strike). No cap.
  • +15 Reflect Physical (45 SP) — bounces melee damage back. Powerful with 220 dex instaheal.
  • +20 Spell Damage Increase (40 SP) — boosts weapon spell hits (most levelled weapons have several).
  • +25 Enhance Potions (50 SP) — with stacked EP, potions can add ~100 HP/Stam/Mana.

Total: 303 SP per slot (fits comfortably in a Normal weapon with a few Leveling Deeds).

How to Use

Plan your UO:EX relayer builds across all equipment slots.

1. Select a Slot

  • Weapon and Shield tabs are colored differently. The rest are armor slots.
  • A green dot means the slot is configured. Strikethrough means unequipped.

2. Choose a Weapon or Item

  • Preset Weapons — known weapons with base stats, filtered by slot type.
  • Notable Items — fixed-stat items (no leveling). Luck Deeds still apply.
  • Custom Weapon / Clothing — input any stats, save as reusable presets. Use Edit/Delete to modify saved items.

3. Deeds & SP

  • Leveling Deeds (up to 10) add bonus SP per deed.
  • Weapon Change Deed removes Arti-Tag (2x SP cost). Weapon Type Change Deed changes weapon abilities (weapon slot only).
  • Use 0 / - / + / MAX buttons to allocate SP. The Cap column shows distance from gear cap.
  • Bold attributes on armor are recommended to always max.

4. Totals Summary

  • Base Stats — enter your naked STR/DEX/INT and stat cap.
  • Character Status — shows calculated HP/Stam/Mana, resists (capped at 70), and Luck.
  • Buffs — toggle spells/effects (Protection, Bless, Vampiric Embrace, Angelic Faith, potions) to see their impact.
  • Unequip — toggle slots to see what you'd lose. Click column headers in the summary table to toggle.

5. Shopping List

  • Tallies all deeds (Relayer, Leveling, Luck, etc.) with ED costs. Check them off as purchased.

6. Save & Export

  • Auto-saves to browser storage. Use Export/Import JSON to back up or share builds.
