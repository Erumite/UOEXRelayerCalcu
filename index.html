How to Use

Plan your UO:EX relayer builds across all armor slots and your weapon.

1. Select a Slot

  • Click a tab to configure that slot. The Weapon tab is for your equipped weapon; the rest are armor slots.
  • A green dot on a tab means that slot is configured.

2. Choose a Weapon or Item

  • Preset Weapons (Titan's Hammer, etc.) are artifact weapons with known base stats ready to level.
  • Notable Items (Spirit of Totem, etc.) have fixed stats and no SP to spend, but you can apply Luck Deeds.
  • Custom Weapon lets you input a looted/found weapon's base stats. Save it as a preset for reuse.

3. Configure Deeds & SP

  • Leveling Deeds add bonus SP (first 9 give an extra level of SP each).
  • Luck Deeds add +100 Luck each (capped at 400 total including base).
  • Arti-tagged weapons cost 2x SP per attribute. Check Weapon Change Deed to remove the tag (200 ED).
  • Allocate your SP across attributes. The bar shows remaining SP budget.

4. Track Totals & Shopping

  • Totals Summary shows the sum of each attribute across all slots vs gear caps.
  • Shopping List tallies all deeds needed with ED costs. Check them off as you purchase.

5. Save & Export

  • Changes auto-save to browser storage. Use Export/Import JSON to back up or share builds.
